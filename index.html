What do you get?

Simplicity

Emoty has easy to use and clean interface with focus on user's needs. Only the most important features are given to user's attention.

Freedom

Emoty is free in every meaning. No cost, no ads, no censorship and full privacy in one application. It's truly decentralized, like nothing before!

Safety

All data on Emoty is fully encrypted by the most sophisiticated algorithms. For enhanced privacy Emoty can be used over Tor and I2P.
